About Jamal Engineering Works

Jamal Engineering Works is a family-owned precision manufacturing company specializing in a wide range of auto parts — motorcycles, auto rickshaws, tractors and commercial vehicles. With experienced craftsmen and modern equipment, we ensure quality, repeatability and timely delivery.
